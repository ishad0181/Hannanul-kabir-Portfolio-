What I Do

Service & Solutions

Structural Designer And Planner

Combining civil engineering and interior design expertise, they deliver efficient, visually appealing architectural designs. From concept to completion, each project harmonizes with its environment and meets client needs.

Auto Cad And 3D Training

Skilled in AutoCAD and 3D modeling, they provide precise and realistic visualizations for every project. With advanced training, they create detailed layouts and lifelike renders, bringing designs to life and aiding clear communication with clients.

Soil Test

Experienced in soil testing and analysis, I ensure that every project starts with a solid foundation. By assessing soil properties and behavior, I provide critical insights that inform design decisions, enhance safety, and optimize construction methods, guaranteeing the durability and success of each structure.

Electrical Design And Exhibition

I deliver innovative solutions that ensure safety and efficiency in every project. My designs optimize functionality and aesthetic appeal, while my exhibition setups create immersive environments that captivate audiences and highlight technological advancements.

Interior Design And Exhibition

Specializing in interior design and exhibition planning, I create captivating spaces that reflect unique identities and enhance user experiences. By blending aesthetics with functionality, I design interiors that inspire and engage, while meticulously crafting exhibitions that showcase creativity and innovation.

Expert Consultancy Services

Providing expert consultancy services, I guide clients through every phase of their projects with informed insights and strategic solutions. My comprehensive approach encompasses design, planning, and implementation, ensuring that each decision aligns with the client's vision and objectives for success.

About Me

Building A Safer, Stronger Future Through Innovative Design And Engineering Excellence.

I'm Engr. Md Hannanul Kabir (Sayem), an extraordinary civil engineer and structural designer dedicated to merging academic knowledge with practical expertise. With a Diploma in Civil from SIPI and a B.Sc in Civil from UCTC, I possess a deep understanding of engineering principles. As the owner and managing partner of SEM Construction & Interior, I lead with a visionary approach, transforming innovative concepts into remarkable structures. My commitment to quality and attention to detail set me apart in the industry, driving projects to success with creativity and precision. With a passion for excellence, I continually push the boundaries of design and construction, shaping a brighter future in the built environment.
